Agricola Studios

Hello There

Founded in 2018, Agricola Studios offers a wide variety of services to help document and create multi-media excellence in video production and photography.

Our staff is led by Kevin Agricola, President and Owner. Kevin started Agricola Studios out of a lifelong desire to help people document and tell their own story. Kevin holds a degree of Associate of Applied Science from Chattahoochee Technical College with a specialization in Television Production Technology and FAA Pilot License for Commercial Drone Operation.

His experience includes five years of media and 14 years of service in both customer and non-profit sectors. Kevin’s years in these sectors have provided a passion for creating high quality videos. His videos range from commercials for small businesses, non-profit, fund-raising and mission trips. Kevin’s experience as a Commercial Drone Pilot adds the capability for a dynamic element to be added to your video production and stand out from the noise of today’s media environment.
